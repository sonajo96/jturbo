SPV Overview & Structure

Strategic investment vehicle for clean energy projects

Strategic SPV Formation

To accelerate execution and unlock growth in India, JTurbo is establishing a Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) — JTurbo Technologies Pvt. Ltd. (JTT) — headquartered in Chennai. This entity will serve as the operational and investment arm for Indian projects.

Investment Opportunity

JTT presents a structured and scalable platform for both JTurbo and new investors to participate in India's growing hydrogen and clean energy landscape. Chennai, a rising tech and industrial hub, will serve as its strategic base.

Talent Acquisition

JTT will recruit top talent from India’s premier institutions and technical ecosystems. Our mission is to cultivate a high-performance culture that fuels innovation, accelerates delivery, and maintains global standards.

Comprehensive Service Offering

JTurbo will channel all its services through the JTT in India, ensuring operational excellence.

Expanding Global Reach

While rooted in India, JTT is positioned to serve both domestic and international markets, enabling JTurbo to tap into new geographies with speed and flexibility.

A Gateway to High-Growth Markets

India’s dynamic energy transition and favorable policies make JTT a compelling destination for strategic investors looking to capitalize on the shift toward hydrogen, LNG, and clean tech.
