Business Model.

Market Entry & Initial Deployments

Phase 1 (Year 1-2)

Revenue Focus

Primarily licensing & equipment sales to establish market presence

Sales Model

Each $2M Licensed unit deployed across 5 plants generates $10M annually

Licensing Agreements

Early adoption by large-scale operators, each paying $ 1M annually per license

Focus on mid-scale and large-scale facilities in key industrial regions. Competitive pricing ensures 40-50% operational cost savings, driving adoption .
