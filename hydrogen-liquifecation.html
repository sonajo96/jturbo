About JTURBO Technology

The JTURBO hydrogen liquefaction system represents a breakthrough in cryogenic technology, utilizing Reverse Brayton cycles for both pre-cooling and liquefaction stages.

  • 50-70% lower energy consumption than conventional systems
  • Patent-protected technology (US 11,391,511 B1)
  • Modular design for scalable deployment
  • Hybrid configurations available with LNG pre-cooling
