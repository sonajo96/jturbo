Technical Risks
Scaling Modular Twin-Expander Technology:

Micro LNG/BIO LNG and lower size Liquid Hydrogen Plants will be first built and proven for feedback improvements before large scale design.

Hydrogen liquefaction technology is not new, but the cycle is new. Vendor verification of the refrigeration profiles and equipment supply guarantee with high efficiency has already been obtained.
