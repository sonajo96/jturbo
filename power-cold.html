Liquid Hydrogen as Power and Cold Energy Buffer

Innovative energy storage solutions leveraging LH₂ and LN₂ for optimal renewable energy utilization

Enabling 24/7 Hydrogen Supply Using Renewable Energy and Cryogenic Buffers

The Challenge

Continuous hydrogen demand—especially for industrial applications—must be met around the clock. But renewable energy sources like solar and wind are only available part of the day. How do we bridge the gap?

Our Solution: Smart Energy Buffering with LH₂ and LN₂

We deploy a high-efficiency hydrogen infrastructure that leverages Liquefied Hydrogen (LH₂) and Liquefied Nitrogen (LN₂) as thermal and energy buffers to ensure uninterrupted supply—even when renewables aren't available.

How It Works

  • ☀️ Daytime: Renewable-Powered Hydrogen Production
    • Renewable electricity is used to produce, liquefy, and store hydrogen (LH₂)
    • Surplus power is stored as clean fuel, ready for use during off-hours
  • 🌙 Nighttime: Hydrogen Release + Cold Energy Recovery
    • Stored LH₂ is vaporized to meet nighttime hydrogen demand
    • Cold energy released during vaporization is captured to liquefy nitrogen (LN₂)
  • 🔄 Next Day: Reusing Stored Cold Energy
    • LN₂ is vaporized during the next hydrogen liquefaction cycle
    • This pre-cools hydrogen gas, drastically improving liquefaction efficiency

Optimized ASU (Air Separation Unit) Integration

  • Nighttime ASU Operation
    • Produces Gaseous Nitrogen (GAN) to:
    • Support ammonia synthesis
    • Recover and store cold energy in LN₂
  • Daytime ASU Operation
    • Supplies GAN for ammonia synthesis
    • Reduces load by deducting GAN obtained from LN₂ vaporization

Key Benefits

  • 24/7 Hydrogen Availability — Even with part-day renewable supply
  • Zero Cold Energy Waste — Full recovery during LH₂ vaporization
  • Lower Operating Costs — Due to energy-efficient cryogenic integration
  • Scalable & Sustainable — Ideal for industrial, grid, and export-scale systems

Revolutionizing Green Hydrogen Infrastructure

By turning cold energy into a reusable asset, and strategically timing gas production and storage, we're setting a new standard in hydrogen efficiency, cost savings, and sustainability.
