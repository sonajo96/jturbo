About

Liquide hydrogen and LNG plays a major role in ongoing energy transition and JTurbo believes in liquefaction efficiency as the key and we invite you to explore it in our website.

We are a stealth-stage climate tech startup pioneering a breakthrough twin-expander liquefaction technology that revolutionizes how hydrogen and LNG/BIO-LNG are liquefied, stored, and transported. Our patented modular systems deliver unmatched energy efficiency, scalability, and environmental benefits—positioning us as a first mover in both traditional and emerging markets

Problem

  • Energy intensive (Hydrogen: 12–15 kWh/kg; LNG/BIO-LNG: 0.50–0.6 kWh/kg).
  • Costly and carbon-heavy, contributing to over $5B in energy losses annually and significant emissions.
  • Inflexible to smaller-scale applications or decentralized infrastructure, especially in emerging economies and remote locations.

Solution

  • Hydrogen SEC of 5.2 kWh/kg (50% lower than market).
  • LNG/BIO-LNG energy consumption of 0.30 kWh/kg, with a 40% efficiency gain.
  • Modular, refrigerant-flexible systems that reduce CAPEX and shorten deployment time by 50%.
  • 15–25% CO₂ reduction, supporting net-zero goals and enabling carbon credit monetization.
