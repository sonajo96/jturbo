Company Overview - JTurbo Engineering & Technology LLC

Jturbo provides hydrogen liquefaction (LH2) and storage solutions for data centers, industrial facilities, and mobility applications.

Hydrogen Liquefaction (LH2) More efficient than Compressed Hydrogen (CH2) or Batteries, leveraging renewable energy forlower CAPEX & OPEX

How the System Works

Daytime: Electrolyzers produce & liquefy hydrogen, storing it in cryogenic tanks.
Nighttime: LH2 vaporizes to power gas turbines or fuel cells, providing continuous, green electricity and feedstock to industrial plants.

Reduces CAPEX & OPEX by 30% compared to CH2 storage.

Provides data center cooling without any additional cost.
